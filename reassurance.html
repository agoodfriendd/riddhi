I'm Not Going Anywhere.

I know after everything I put you through, those words probably feel hollow. Like something anyone could say. But I need you to know I mean it, not because it's a nice thing to say at the end of all this, but because it's genuinely true.

I've sat with myself a lot lately. More than I'm comfortable admitting. And the thing that hurts the most isn't guilt, it's the thought of you thinking I don't actually care. Because I do. More than I ever let on, more than I showed you, more than I probably deserved to.

You're not someone I'm going to get tired of. You're not someone I'm going to replace. You're not someone I'm keeping around until something better comes along. There is no "better." There's just you, and there's everything I almost threw away.

And I want to be specific about what "not going anywhere" actually means, because I think you deserve more than just a promise:

  • It means when things get hard between us, when you're upset with me, when I mess up, when communication breaks down, I don't disappear. I stay, I own it, I work through it with you.
  • It means your bad days don't scare me away. Your moods, your walls, your moments of not knowing what you want. None of that makes me want to leave. It makes me want to stay closer.
  • It means I'm not here for the easy version of you. I'm here for all of it: tired you, anxious you, the you that doesn't have it together, the you that needs space but also needs someone to not leave.
  • It means I choose you on the days it's easy, and I choose you on the days it's not. Choosing you isn't a sacrifice for me. It's the only thing that actually makes sense.
  • It means even when you push back, even when you test it, even when you're waiting for me to prove you right and disappear. I won't. I refuse to be another person who confirmed your worst fears about people.

You've been let down before. By people who said things and didn't mean them. I know that. I know it's not fair to just ask you to believe me, you have every reason not to. So I'm not asking you to believe words anymore. I'm asking you to watch what I do.

I know trust isn't rebuilt in a day. I know this apology, this whole thing, is just a beginning, not a resolution. But it's real. Every word of it. And I'm willing to take as long as it takes.

You're stuck with me, Riddhi. Not because you can't get rid of me, but because I genuinely, honestly don't want to be anywhere else.

You are also not going anywhere. Who is going to go to Pizza Hut with me if you leave?

Always yours,
no matter what you decide.
