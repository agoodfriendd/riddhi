Playing with Your Feelings

Your feelings were never a game. They were real, vulnerable, and precious. And I disrespected them completely.

I made you feel foolish for caring. I made you question whether you could trust your own heart. That is unforgivable.

Your feelings deserved to be cherished, protected, and honored. Instead, I mocked them for my own amusement. I hurt you, and I can never take that back.
