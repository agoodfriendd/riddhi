One Last Thing

Riddhi,

There is something I still need to say. Something I need you to actually hear.

I am genuinely sorry for hurting you, Riddhi.
I am sorry for threatening you. You never deserved that. Not even a little. Not even for a second.

There is no reason good enough, no frustration deep enough, no argument heated enough that could ever justify what I said or did. I was wrong. Completely, WRONG. And I know that.

I want you to know something clearly: you are safe. You have nothing to fear from me. I will never say or do anything like that again. That is not who I want to be, and it is certainly not how I want you to feel around me, not afraid, not unsure, never uncertain about whether you are okay.

I have always cared for you. I still do. That has never changed, and it never will. If anything ever happens to you, if you are ever in pain, scared, or unsure, I will always be there. Without question, without hesitation.

I promise I will never hurt you again.

You are like a sister to me. And I mean that in every real sense of the word. You always will be. No argument, no distance, no silence will ever change that. I am your brother, and I always will be.

I keep thinking about it.

What will I lose if I have you?
What will I have if I lose you, Riddhi?

The answer scares me. Because the truth is, you mean the world to me. And I refuse to let my worst moments define what we are to each other.

The best sister.
Love you.
